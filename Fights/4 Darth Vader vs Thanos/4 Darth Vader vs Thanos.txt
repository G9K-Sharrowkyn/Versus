- Powers: Force Crush (internal crushing), Tutaminis (energy absorption), precognition, Force Scream.
- Tools: Lightsaber (Kyber-focus), armor with pulse generators augmenting movements.
- Weaknesses: Dependence on life-support systems. Thanos can use EMP or matter manipulation to disable them.
- Weaknesses: Power scale limited to galactic tier (cannot destroy planets with bare hands).
