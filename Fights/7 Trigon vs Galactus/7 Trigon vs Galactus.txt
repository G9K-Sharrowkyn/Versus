3. Character A Feats

- Advantage: Scaling to Spectre. Ranked by Monitors as a threat on par with the Spectre, placing him in the highest hierarchy of DC power. Possesses the "Heart of Darkness," containing the evil from hundreds of galaxies.

10. Character A Powers / Tools / Weaknesses
- Powers: Reality Warping, absorption of billions of souls, Death Stare, Mid-Godly regeneration.
- Tools: Staff of power, Heart of Darkness, Trophy Room full of artifacts from defeated gods.
- Weaknesses: Vulnerability to very high-level magic and holy grounds (Theology).
- Weaknesses: Needs billions of souls to physically cross interdimensional barriers at full power.

11. Character A Raw Feats
- Stomps the JLA and Teen Titans simultaneously while residing in another dimension.
- Monitor ranks his power on par with the Spectre (God's Spirit of Vengeance).
- Ruled a planet at age 1, destroyed it at age 6, conquered a dimension at age 30.
- Killed his creators (The Divine) and crafted their bodies into his robes.
- Possesses Trophies from DC's greatest heroes (Superman's cape, Fate's helmet).

12. Character B Powers / Tools / Weaknesses
- Powers: Power Cosmic (manipulation of matter, energy, time), telepathy superior to Odin's, immortality.
- Tools: Ultimate Nullifier (existence-erasing weapon), Worldship Taa II (solar system-sized).
- Weaknesses: Hunger—his power drops drastically if he doesn't consume planetary energy regularly.
- Weaknesses: Hubris—often ignores attacks as insignificant, which Trigon can exploit.

13. Character B Raw Feats
- Kills a Celestial (Cosmic God) with a single shot when well-fed.
- His battle with Scrier and the Other threatened the destruction of all universes.
- Takes a headbutt from Odin and regenerates instantly (Odin falls into sleep).
- Ultimate Nullifier erases beings from the very fabric of history (Timeline Erasure).
- Must exist for the universe not to collapse into non-existence (brother of Eternity).
