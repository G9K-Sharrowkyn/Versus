## 0. Hook
Atrocitus vs World Breaker Hulk is not a simple "hax beats brick" fight. Atrocitus owns the cleaner opening toolkit, but Hulk owns the cleaner end condition if the fight turns repetitive. The whole matchup is a test of whether rage can be externally managed before it becomes a self-feeding gamma loop.

## 1. Introduction and methodology
This paper uses a scan-first workflow built from ComicVine respect material, Reddit respect-thread style sourcing, and DC/Marvel Fandom only as continuity and issue-location support. The locked versions are `Atrocitus (Butcher)` and `World Breaker Hulk`, using the project's 9-category stat model and curated showcase lists instead of exhaustive dumps. Atrocitus is intentionally kept version-pure to the Butcher-host framing, which makes his clean opponent record narrower than a broader composite Red Lantern read.

## 2. Character introduction
### 2.1 Atrocitus
Atrocitus is the cleaner starter. He brings flight, plasma, blood ritual control, and a version-defining relationship with the Butcher that makes rage manipulation more than flavor text. He wants the fight externalized, ranged, and structured before Hulk can collapse it into a contact loop.

### 2.2 World Breaker Hulk
World Breaker Hulk is the opposite. He is simpler, but his simplicity is the point: survive the first layer, close distance, and turn every exchange into a stronger next exchange. He gets more dangerous when control fails and less dependent on elegance the dirtier the fight becomes.

## 4. Origin and profile
Atrocitus is the more deliberate fighter. Even in a rage-centered version, he still wants the fight managed through range, ritual, and pressure sequencing. Hulk is less versatile but more punishing once the fight stops being managed. That contrast is why this matchup is deceptive: the smarter, broader kit belongs to Atrocitus, but the cleaner real-world loop belongs to Hulk if the structure breaks.

## 5. Powers and weaknesses
### Atrocitus
Atrocitus brings rage plasma, blood magic, flight, construct pressure, and the Butcher containment angle that gives him the best opening hax route in the matchup. His problem is that his safest wins are front-loaded. If Hulk is still functioning after the first control cycle, Atrocitus starts feeding the exact emotional engine he wanted to steer.

### Hulk
World Breaker Hulk brings gamma escalation, absurd regeneration, shockwave output, contact pressure, and the stamina to keep repeating the same exchange until the other plan breaks. His weakness is that he has fewer non-physical answers if he cannot force contact, but that weakness matters only if Atrocitus can keep the fight clean for a very long time.

### Swing factor
The swing factor is whether Atrocitus can keep Hulk in an externally managed rage state instead of helping create a self-sustaining gamma escalation loop.

## 8. Hypothetical fight - step by step
### 8.1 Opening
Atrocitus opens the right way: flight, plasma, blood magic, and a deliberate attempt to keep Hulk in a managed emotional state instead of a physical one. Hulk's first job is simple and brutal. He tanks the first layer, shortens the distance, and tests whether Atrocitus can actually stop the second contact instead of just the first one.

### 8.2 Mid-fight
The middle of the fight is where the matchup becomes honest. If Atrocitus is still steering the rage state, the paper edge starts to matter. If Hulk is merely getting angrier and still closing distance, then the hax layer has failed in the one way that matters, because every partial success becomes fuel instead of progress.

### 8.3 Turning points
- The first moment Atrocitus fails to turn a clean ranged stop into a durable reset.
- The first time Hulk re-enters contact range without visibly losing functional momentum.
- The point where rage manipulation starts feeding Hulk's escalation more than it limits him.
- The point where Hulk's regeneration makes Atrocitus's earlier successes functionally temporary.

### 8.4 Source verdict distribution
- **Explicit:** `Comic Vine battle thread - Atrocitus vs World Breaker Hulk` (`https://comicvine.gamespot.com/forums/battles-7/atrocitus-vs-world-breaker-hulk-672580/`) leans Hulk once repeat contact and regeneration are treated as the real deciding loop rather than the opening exchange.  
- **Derived:** Fresh feat synthesis from the version-pure Atrocitus file plus the broader World Breaker Hulk record still points to the same shape: Atrocitus starts cleaner, Hulk finishes cleaner.

## 9. Minority perspective
The real pro-Atrocitus argument is not that he is stronger overall. It is that he is faster to his best answers. If he keeps the fight airborne, externalized, and blood-magic first, Hulk may never get the second meaningful contact sequence that the whole gamma loop needs.

## 10. Base verdict
**World Breaker Hulk, 6.5/10.** Atrocitus wins the paper comparison and owns the neater opening route, but Hulk owns the better repeatable finish. Once Atrocitus has to prove the same control sequence twice, Hulk's regeneration and contact escalation make the fight tilt away from the cleaner profile and toward the uglier, more reliable one.

## 11. Conditional verdict
1. **Atrocitus maintains distance/control:** Atrocitus `6/10`  
   If his plasma, blood ritual pressure, and flight control keep Hulk externally managed from start to finish, his better speed and hax let him steer the fight before it becomes a loop.

2. **Hulk forces repeat contact:** Hulk `8/10`  
   If Hulk gets back into range more than once, the fight stops being about Atrocitus's first answer and starts being about whether those answers permanently solve anything. That is the branch Hulk wins hard.

## 12. Why stats and verdict can diverge
Atrocitus has the better average profile because he has more tools, more control, and the cleaner opening game. Hulk still wins more real fights because his finish route is simpler and more repeatable. This matchup is decided by loop control, not by who has the broader kit on paper.

## 13. Closing
This is a real conditional matchup, but not a 50/50. Atrocitus has the cleaner start, yet Hulk has the cleaner long-fight identity. The biggest uncertainty is how much Butcher-locked rage control can truly manage Hulk without feeding the exact escalation it is trying to suppress.
